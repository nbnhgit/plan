Day 1:You Can Do It

Change

  • In order to conquer you need to change some habits.

  • The biggest blockage to this change is in our mindset.

  • Tell yourself you can do it, and you can deal with it. Whatever “it” is you are not alone in it.

Don’t Doubt

  • Sometimes we doubt God out of acting. Or doubt God so much that we don’t get in position.

  • Did you know God is one breath away, and will hear you when you pray.

  • Speak to the root of your mindset, whether that be some trauma you have faced or fear you learn along the way and say you can do it.

Day 2: Posture To Conquer

Scripture

Isaiah 55:1-13 NIV

  2. Why spend money on what is not bread, and your labor on what does not satisfy? Listen, listen to me, and eat what is good, and you will delight in the richest of fare.

  3. Give ear and come to me; listen, that you may live. I will make an everlasting covenant with you, my faithful love promised to David.

  4. See, I have made him a witness to the peoples, a ruler and commander of the peoples.

  5. Surely you will summon nations you know not, and nations you do not know will come running to you, because of the Lord your God, the Holy One of Israel, for he has endowed you with splendor."

6 Seek the Lord while he may be found; call on him while he is near.

  1. Let the wicked forsake their ways and the unrighteous their thoughts. Let them turn to the Lord, and he will have mercy on them, and to our God, for he will freely pardon.

  2. “For my thoughts are not your thoughts, neither are your ways my ways,” declares the Lord.

  3. "As the heavens are higher than the earth, so are my ways higher than your ways and my thoughts than your thoughts.

  4. As the rain and the snow come down from heaven, and do not return to it without watering the earth and making it bud and flourish, so that it yields seed for the sower and bread for the eater,

  5. so is my word that goes out from my mouth: It will not return to me empty, but will accomplish what I desire and achieve the purpose for which I sent it.

  6. You will go out in joy and be led forth in peace; the mountains and hills will burst into song before you, and all the trees of the field will clap their hands.

  7. Instead of the thornbush will grow the juniper, and instead of briers the myrtle will grow. This will be for the Lord’s renown, for an everlasting sign, that will endure forever."

Posture

  • If you have not conquered, you cannot be a conqueror. We need to be realistic in our approach to this idea so that we do not fall into error and become in effective in our efforts.

  • Given the severity of where God is taking us, he said this is the season to get it because we don’t know what tomorrow brings. It’s the season to learn the lesson, to mature, to grow, to do better; the time is now.

  • We will not conquer unless we have the right posture, but with the right posture we can pull from the spiritual into the natural.

Fullness

  • God is always ready to renew his covenant with you. It’s because of that why we have everything we need to conquer. It’s His word, and without it, we will not conquer.

  • Stand fast in the liberty where with God has made us free, and slay self righteousness.

  • The brokenness that we feel at times is a distraction from the goal. Drop off weariness for the Lord is your strength. You will reap what was stolen, you won’t be worn out, and you will no longer almost but will conquer.

Day 3: Called To Conquer

Scripture

2 Peter 1:3-9 NIV

  1. His divine power has given us everything we need for a godly life through our knowledge of him who called us by his own glory and goodness.

  2. Through these he has given us his very great and precious promises, so that through them you may participate in the divine nature, having escaped the corruption in the world caused by evil desires.

  3. For this very reason, make every effort to add to your faith goodness; and to goodness, knowledge;

  4. and to knowledge, self-control; and to self-control, perseverance; and to perseverance, godliness;

  5. and to godliness, mutual affection; and to mutual affection, love.

  6. For if you possess these qualities in increasing measure, they will keep you from being ineffective and unproductive in your knowledge of our Lord Jesus Christ.

  7. But whoever does not have them is nearsighted and blind, forgetting that they have been cleansed from their past sins.

Life In The Balance

  • As the complexity of our life circumstances increase they seem more and more closer to making life or death decisions. This calls for us to learn how to do well with living “life in the balance.”

  • An important element to this balance is growing in grace. Peter made major contributions in this area of our faith highlighted in 2 Peter ch 1. Sometimes we get familiar with doing life in bondage that when we are free we go back to life as we knew it. Let us then keep in mind that growth takes review, and review means to look back at where God has brought you in appreciate but looking for greater and bigger things ahead

Conquered To Conquer

  • There are some things we never want to let happen again. It’s in that emotional place we need to make concrete decisions about how we’d like to progress from this point on. It could be a bad brake-up, a falling class grade, or even an argument that went too far.

  • However, no matter what you are going through, there is mercy, grace, a promise, the prophetic, and power to conquer. This is not the end for you.

It’s In You

  • Often times to conquer it’s not a matter of figuring out what you need, but more of what you already have. In a consumer driven society we fall in the trap of always thinking we need more to do more, however, sometimes you can do more with what you already have.

  • To conquer we must let go of blaming others for our short comings, become obedient, and do things to please God.

  • David was anointed, he had salvation (he knew God), was humble, had a staff, 5 stones, and a sling. He refused the kings armor because he already had what he needed to defeat Goliath. Take a look around, you may find out that you already have what you need to defeat your giants.

Kingdom Way

  • David had a way with words, or maybe he had a way to invite God into the circumstance he was facing. David activated the promise of God in His fight with Goliath. “Today the philistines will know that there is a God in Israel”.

  • God’s favor is constant because it’s a product of God’s love, and is amplified by righteousness, and pureness of heart.

  • Ask God to surround you with people who have vision so that when you are low, you do not completely lose sight of your goals, and that God is for you not against you.

Fight By Faith

  • Faith is a reliable source to keep us going even when it seems as if we’re not winning.

  • Faith renews our thoughts

  • Faith is what we use when self thinks it’s all over

Get Out Of Hiding

  • God wants to fight for you

  • God wants you to know who you are
